What is Maris Stella College

Maris Stella College, Negombo in Sri Lanka is a semi-government Catholic school conducted by Marist brothers. Started by Marist brothers came from France and formed in 1922. The school is celibarating century in 2022.

Current Governance

The school is controlled by Marist Brothers. The present principal of Maris Stella College Negombo is Rev. Bro. Sunanda Alwis.

History of us

In 1918 four Marist Brothers came to Negombo, Sri Lanka and took over the management of St Mary's College. In 1922, the school moved to its present site and assumed the new name of Maris Stella College. The history of the Primary School goes back to 1922 when Bro. Lewis was in charge of the elementary School.

Our Mission

Enabling a young man with a discerning spirit and Marist Educational culture to have a balanced personality so that he can respond to the signs of the times to make a real difference in our world.

Our Motto

ITER PARATUTUM - meaning of our motto is "show us the way". School of light caring for life, Inviting all to be a light in the moment of darkness
